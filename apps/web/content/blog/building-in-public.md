From day one, we decided to **build Acme in public**. Our roadmap is open, our changelog is public,
and we share the messy middle as much as the polished launches.

## Three reasons it works

1. **Faster feedback.** When customers see what’s coming, they tell us what matters before we ship.
2. **Trust.** Transparency about what we’re working on — and what we got wrong — builds real trust.
3. **Momentum.** Shipping in the open keeps us honest and keeps us moving.

Building in public isn’t always comfortable. But every time we’ve shared early, the product got
better for it.
