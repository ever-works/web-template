We started Acme with a simple belief: **building something great shouldn’t take months of setup.**

Too often, the gap between an idea and a real, live product is filled with plumbing — auth, hosting,
design systems, deployment. By the time you’re ready to talk to customers, the momentum is gone.

## What Acme gives you

- A beautiful, fast foundation that works on day one
- Sensible defaults so you can focus on your product, not the scaffolding
- Room to grow — customize everything as you scale
